Welcome to Nora International Secondary School

Enriching Education. Fostering Community

Nora International Secondary School is rooted in the belief that every student should be challenged, engaged, and supported in a learning environment that places no limits on what they can achieve. We have created an educational space for profoundly gifted learners to flex their intellect, explore their passions, build their sense of self and community and learn alongside their intellectual peers.

Connecting highly gifted youth to their potential.

Teachers in Nora International Secondary School guide profoundly gifted youth to learn at a level that aligns with their abilities, in a nurturing environment that recognizes their potential and provides them with a sense of ownership over their education, abilities and individual growth.

Admissions

Nora International Secondary School is specifically designed to meet the needs of profoundly gifted students. Our accredited classes group students by ability rather than age, offering middle and high school level curriculum. Nora International Secondary School is centered on a rigorous academic environment where students can thrive among their intellectual peers. Our unique approach to learning has attracted families from across the country and globe.

Application

The 2022-2023 application deadline has passed. The 2023-2024 application will be available this fall.

Academics

We match each student's needs to a curriculum driven by ability grouping and flexibility, not a one-size-fits-all lockstep approach to education. An individualized education for each profoundly gifted middle and high school student gives each an opportunity to learn to the full extent of their abilities in a supportive environment with intellectual peers of similar age.

Student Life

Nora International Secondary School is designed to be much more than just a school. At the school students experience friendship with intellectual peers, extra-curricular activities, community involvement and more.
