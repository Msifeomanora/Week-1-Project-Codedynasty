About Us

Who We Serve

We guide profoundly gifted junior and secondary school students. Profoundly gifted students score in the 99.9th percentile on IQ and achievement tests, and think and learn differently than other students.

What We Do

We provide highly gifted young people with an education matched to their abilities in an environment with intellectual peers that supports their unique strengths and intellect. Our vision is to challenge, nurture, educate and inspire our brightest students so they can change the world.

Our History

In 2006, Nora International Secondary school opened as the first affordable private school of its kind for profoundly gifted junior and senior secondary school students. In 2016, a fully accredited online campus was added to Nora international offerings.

Message from Ifeoma Nora (Founder ~ Nora International)

I am often asked why I support our nation’s brightest students with a special school like Nora International Secondary School. My answer is two-fold: It’s the right thing to do for the students, and it’s the right thing to do for our nation.
